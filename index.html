Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Vynix Chat ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our service.

Last updated: December 29, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information:

  • Account Information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Guest Access: If you continue as a guest, we create an anonymous account with no personal identifiers.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymized data about how you interact with the app (e.g., features used) to improve the service.
  • Messages: Your chat messages are stored securely and are only accessible to you and the recipients.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain the chat service
  • Authenticate your identity
  • Enable real-time messaging
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Respond to support requests

3. Data Storage and Security

All data is stored using secure Firebase services provided by Google. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

Messages are end-to-end accessible only by participants. We do not read or scan your private conversations.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • With your explicit consent
  • To comply with legal requirements
  • With service providers (like Google Firebase) that help us operate the app under strict confidentiality

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Delete your account and associated data (contact support)
  • Opt out of non-essential data collection

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes within the app.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@vynixchat.com

Thank you for trusting Vynix Chat.
