Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 15, 2025

BanterBox ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our social media and messaging platform ("BanterBox" or the "Service"). By using BanterBox, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the Service.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve the Service. The types of information we collect include:

a. Personal Information

  • Account Information: When you sign up, we collect your email address, username, and optional profile details (e.g., bio, profile picture) via Firebase Authentication.
  • User Content: Posts, stories, messages, comments, and media (images, voice notes) you upload are stored via Firebase Realtime Database and Cloudinary.
  • Presence Data: We track your online/offline status and "Last seen" timestamps to enable real-time interactions.
  • Notifications: Data related to likes, comments, follows, and verification status is stored to provide real-time notifications.

b. Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with BanterBox, such as pages visited, features used (e.g., likes, comments, searches), and timestamps.
  • Device Information: We may collect device type, browser type, IP address, and operating system to optimize the Service for your device.
  • WebRTC Data: For video calls, we process temporary connection data (e.g., ICE candidates) via WebRTC and STUN servers to establish peer-to-peer communication.

c. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies to maintain your session and preferences (e.g., chat wallpaper settings). Essential cookies are required for the Service to function, and no optional tracking cookies are used.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide the Service: Authenticate users, display posts and stories, enable messaging and video calls, and deliver notifications.
  • Personalize Your Experience: Show relevant content (e.g., posts from followed users) and customize features like chat wallpapers.
  • Improve the Service: Analyze usage patterns to enhance performance, fix bugs, and optimize responsiveness.
  • Ensure Security: Detect and prevent unauthorized access or abuse (e.g., oversized uploads or invalid media formats).
  • Communicate: Send service-related updates, such as verification badge awards or notification alerts.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information as follows:

  • With Other Users: Your username, profile picture, posts, stories, and comments are visible to other users based on your actions (e.g., public posts or private messages to friends).
  • Service Providers: We use third-party services to operate BanterBox:
    • Firebase (Google): Handles authentication, real-time database, and hosting.
    • Cloudinary: Stores and serves media uploads (images, voice notes, profile pictures).
    • WebRTC STUN Servers: Facilitate video call connectivity.
    These providers process data under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, such as in response to a court order or to protect the safety of users or the public.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Storage: Personal information and user content are stored in Firebase Realtime Database (United States) and Cloudinary (global servers). Presence data and temporary WebRTC data are not persistently stored.
  • Security Measures:
    • Firebase Authentication ensures secure access.
    • HTTPS encryption protects data in transit.
    • Media uploads are validated for size (max 10MB for chat media, 5MB for profile pictures) and format (JPEG, PNG, GIF).
    • Access to user data is restricted to authenticated users via Firebase security rules.
  • Retention: We retain your data as long as your account is active. Deleted posts, messages, or accounts are removed from our systems, though some data (e.g., "This message was deleted" placeholders) may persist for continuity.

5. Your Choices and Rights

  • Access and Update: You can view and edit your profile (username, bio, profile picture) via the Profile tab’s Edit Profile modal.
  • Delete Content: You can delete your posts or messages, which are removed from Firebase and Cloudinary (where applicable).
  • Account Deletion: Contact us at support@banterbox.app to request account deletion. We will remove your personal information within 30 days, subject to legal obligations.
  • Notifications: You can manage notification preferences in the Settings section.
  • Cookies: You can disable cookies via your browser settings, but this may affect core functionality (e.g., session persistence).

6. Accessibility and Children’s Privacy

  • Accessibility: BanterBox includes ARIA labels and semantic HTML to support screen readers, ensuring accessibility for all users.
  • Children’s Privacy: BanterBox is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If we learn such data has been collected, we will delete it promptly.

7. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase: Authentication and database services are provided by Google Firebase. See their Privacy Policy.
  • Cloudinary: Media storage is handled by Cloudinary. See their Privacy Policy.
  • WebRTC: Video calls use open-source WebRTC with public STUN servers, which do not store personal data.

8. International Data Transfers

BanterBox operates globally, and your data may be processed in the United States or other regions where Firebase and Cloudinary servers are located. We ensure compliance with applicable data protection laws for cross-border transfers.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify you via the Service or email (if provided) of significant changes. The updated policy will be effective as of the "Last Updated" date above.
